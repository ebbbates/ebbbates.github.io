Target Audience

The target audience is 20 - 40 year old men and women. This age range can cover anyone from college students, to parents with children. The information will be relevant to those who are seeking opportunities to go out, have fun, and explore. It will contain weather reports, as well as information on local restaurants, recreational activities, and events. The site has a modern, clean, and straightforward look, with bright colors for more visual appeal. It will be mobile-friendly, as the expectation is that most users will be visiting the site on their phone.

Persona 1

David is a 35 year old, middle class, married father of 2 elementary aged children. He and his wife are planning a day trip to Bear Lake after a long work week. They watch the weather each day leading up to the weekend to make sure weather conditions will be favorable. They hope to rent a paddle board in Fish Haven to take to North Beach State Park. His wife wants to take the kids to a local restaurant for famous raspberry shakes, and asks him to find one with good reviews. A quick glance at our site offers up-to-date weather information, as well as links to local restaurants and water equipment rentals.

Persona 2

Haylee is a 21 year old college student planning a spontaneous, weekend camping trip with a group of friends. As she waits for her last class to end for the day, she uses her phone to search weather conditions in Soda Springs. On our site, she sees that the weather will be perfect for a camping trip, and finds the links to local campsites and hiking destinations very convenient. A quick text to her group of friends confirms the weekend's plans, all before her class ends!

Scenarios

  • What is the weather going to be like at Bear Lake on Saturday?
  • What campgrounds are near Soda Springs?
  • What public beaches are there at Bear Lake?

  • Where can I rent a paddleboard at Bear Lake?
  • What is the best hike in Soda Springs?
