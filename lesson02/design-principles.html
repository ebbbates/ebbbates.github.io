Visual Hierarchy

  • SmugMug
  • smugmug.com

In the example of Visual Hierarchy, SmugMug uses three main elements right on the first page, to grab the attention of the customer.

  1. My eye went straight to the large white text first: a short and sweet summary of what they do.
  2. I then looked at the call to action button "GET STARTED FREE".
  3. Lastly, in case the customer needs more information, the smaller text gives a bit more information to the customer.

White Space

  • Amy Rau Photography
  • amyrauphotography.com

Amy Rau Photography uses White Space and Clean Design to create a simple and easy to navigate, website.

The white space and clean design, also creates a site whose main emphasis is the actual photography and how to contact the photographer. It allows the customer to focus on the purpose of the site, without unnecessary distraction.

PARC: Proximity

  • Old Navy
  • oldnavy.com

Old Navy uses Proximity to make it simple for customers to know which description and price is associated with each picture/product.

The picture, description, and price are near each other to indicate relationship. Each product as a whole (picture, description, price) has enough space around it to separate it from the next product.
